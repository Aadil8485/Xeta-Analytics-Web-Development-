Barbell Bicep Curl

The barbell Curl is a Classic and effective bicep exercise that target the muscles in
the front of your upper arms, specifically the biceps branchil.

Bicep Development

he barbell bicep curl specifically
targets the biceps brachii,
contributing to their development
and definition

Strengthen Building

The exercise allows for heavy
resistance, promoting strength gains
in the biceps and surrounding
muscle groups

Increased Muscle Engagement

By maintaining strict form and
controlling the weight, you ensure that
the biceps are doing the majority of
the work, leading to better muscle
engagement and growth.

Compound Movement

While primarily an isolation exercise for
the biceps, the barbell curl involves other
muscles to a lesser extent, such as the
forearms, shoulders, and upper back.
This makes it a compound movement
that engages multiple muscle groups
